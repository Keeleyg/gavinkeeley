Lead On Purpose

Intentional Leadership in a World of Chaos

Here's the truth I've figured out: most leaders have no idea what they're doing. They were never trained. They got promoted because they were good at something else, handed a team, and left to figure it out.

Lead On Purpose doesn't just challenge conventional leadership wisdom. It exposes why it was always destined to fail. Friedman's doctrine is dead. Classical management theory was built for a world that no longer exists.

"You are currently experiencing the fastest rate of change in your life, but it will be the slowest you experience for the rest of your lifetime."

How prepared do you feel to lead through that? This book offers a different path: building an invincible culture that replaces dogma with evidence and pretence with competence.

Evidence first. Values always. Dogma never.

Who I Am

I've spent forty years watching leaders fail in predictable ways. Not because they lacked intelligence or ambition, but because they were never actually trained to lead. I wrote this book because I got tired of staying quiet about it.

My scientific training established an evidence-based approach to decision-making and a commitment to holding strong views loosely. I've held executive roles at Suncorp, Aviva PLC, and Hollard Insurance, and founded and led technology companies across North America, Europe, and Asia.

Today, I'm Partner at Circle Leadership Global and Tractor Ventures. I advise boards and leadership teams, serve as Adjunct Professor at Queensland University of Technology, and have called the Sunshine Coast home with my family for the last 20 years.

I remain convinced that leadership can be learned, and that most of what passes for leadership wisdom is comfortable fiction.
